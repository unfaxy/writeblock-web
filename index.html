Why lock anything

It's not willpower.
It's structure.

“I'll write every day” never survives a dopamine app. So we skip the reminder and use a lock. Until you write, the app you most want to open stays shut. Non-negotiable.

How it works

Three steps. Done.

A minute to set up. Two to write. That's it.

01 — Pick

Pick your apps

Choose which dopamine apps to pause, and when. Defaults to 10 PM — set it anywhere from 6 PM to just before midnight.

02 — Lock

Night falls, apps lock

Your apps go quiet. Add a photo or a voice memo if you like, but 50 characters of text is the only way back in.

03 — Open

Write, and it opens

About three sentences — 50 characters. Your streak ticks up, your apps come back. Skip it and everything unlocks on its own at 4 AM. Nothing stays locked for good.
